About Us

We are two passionate teenagers driven by a shared vision: to create meaningful impact in the world around us. From a young age, we’ve been fascinated by the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) global blueprint for a more sustainable future. Yet, while these goals inspired us, they often felt distant, almost like ideals rather than something we could actively contribute to.

Determined to bridge that gap, we founded “17 Steps Forward” an initiative designed to turn the SDGs into real, tangible actions in our community. Our approach is simple: for each of the 17 goals, we design activities that engage and empower people to participate in building a better world.

Through workshops, hands-on activities, talks, and interactive booths, we tackle issues ranging from social justice and peace, to climate action, education, and equality. Every project is purposefully aligned with a specific SDG, ensuring that our efforts create lasting change that extends beyond awareness into meaningful action.

Our initiative is about cultivating a culture of responsibility, empathy, and proactive engagement. We believe that even small actions, when done consistently, can ripple outward and make a real difference.

With “17 Steps Forward”, we aim not only to inspire others to take part, but also to show that age is no barrier to making an impact.

We are always eager to collaborate with schools, organizations, and community groups to make a greater impact. If you would like to offer a venue, partner with us, or share ideas for future projects, please don’t hesitate to contact us. We would be more than happy to explore opportunities together to bring the SDGs to life.
